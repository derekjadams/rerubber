OUR RECLEAN™ TECHNOLOGY

11 Steps. Medical-Grade Standards.

Our proprietary sanitization process meets or exceeds FDA-adjacent quality benchmarks for post-consumer latex reconditioning.

1

Collection & Sorting

Returned items are sorted by material type and condition grade at our USDA-Adjacent Sanitization Facility.

2

Industrial Wash Cycle

Triple-wash in pharmaceutical-grade solution. Residual organic matter is completely removed.

3

UV-C Sterilization

Medical-grade ultraviolet light exposure eliminates 99.97% of surface pathogens.

4

Latex Reconditioning

Proprietary elasticity restoration treatment returns material to near-original tensile strength.

5

Re-Lubrication & Sealing

Fresh medical-grade lubricant applied. Individually sealed in tamper-evident packaging.

6

Pressure Integrity Test

Every unit inflated to 40L capacity. 99.7% pass rate. Failures are composted responsibly.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We Get It. You Have Questions.

Transparency is at the heart of everything we do. Here are the questions our members ask most.

Absolutely. Our proprietary 11-step ReClean™ process includes industrial UV-C sterilization, pharmaceutical-grade washing, latex reconditioning, and an individual pressure integrity test. Every unit must pass a 40-liter inflation test before being repackaged. Our quality benchmarks meet or exceed FDA-adjacent standards for post-consumer latex products.

We've developed our quality protocols in parallel with FDA guidelines for latex medical devices. While the FDA does not currently have a specific regulatory framework for reconditioned condoms, our standards are modeled on their existing benchmarks for single-use latex products. We're actively engaged with regulatory bodies to help establish this emerging category.

Each monthly shipment includes a prepaid return mailer. Simply place your used items inside, seal the mailer, and drop it in any USPS mailbox. Our sanitization team will process, recondition, and redistribute them within 5-7 business days. The return mailer is leak-proof, odor-sealed, and fully compliant with USPS biohazard shipping guidelines.

Every reconditioned condom is individually assessed and graded based on post-processing condition. Grade A+ ("Premium Donor Stock") indicates single previous use with zero visual or structural imperfections. Grade A ("Like New") indicates minimal prior use with full structural integrity. Grade B+ ("Gently Used") indicates minor cosmetic wear that does not affect performance. All grades pass the same pressure integrity test.
