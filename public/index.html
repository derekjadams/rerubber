We've developed our quality protocols in parallel with FDA guidelines for latex medical devices. While the FDA does not currently have a specific regulatory framework for reconditioned condoms, our standards are modeled on their existing benchmarks for single-use latex products. We're actively engaged with regulatory bodies to help establish this emerging category.
Each monthly shipment includes a prepaid return mailer. Simply place your used items inside, seal the mailer, and drop it in any USPS mailbox. Our sanitization team will process, recondition, and redistribute them within 5-7 business days. The return mailer is leak-proof, odor-sealed, and fully compliant with USPS biohazard shipping guidelines.
Every reconditioned condom is individually assessed and graded based on post-processing condition. Grade A+ ("Premium Donor Stock") indicates single previous use with zero visual or structural imperfections. Grade A ("Like New") indicates minimal prior use with full structural integrity. Grade B+ ("Gently Used") indicates minor cosmetic wear that does not affect performance. All grades pass the same pressure integrity test.
